# Data policy

This repository is intended for public, reusable workflow development. It must not contain clinical or collaborator-sensitive data.

## Allowed

- synthetic examples;
- public datasets with clear reuse permission;
- scripts;
- environment files;
- workflow documentation;
- small toy outputs generated from synthetic or public data.

## Not allowed

- patient data;
- sample identifiers;
- hospital metadata;
- raw clinical POD5, FASTQ, BAM, or CRAM files;
- unpublished collaborator datasets;
- internal meeting notes;
- institutional screenshots;
- grant documents or internal budgets;
- assay performance results before permission or publication.

## Grey zone

Ask before publishing:

- exact wet-lab SOPs from an institution or collaborator;
- real primer or probe designs tied to unpublished assay work;
- server paths, usernames, or cluster-specific internal configuration;
- real QC plots from project samples.

## Practical rule

If a file answers "what happened to a real person, sample, hospital, collaborator, or unpublished project?", keep it out of this repository.
